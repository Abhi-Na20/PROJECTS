​​The 2025 Jeep Compass is a compact SUV that blends modern design with versatile performance and advanced features. It is powered by a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine producing 200 horsepower and 221 lb-ft of torque, paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission. All-wheel drive is standard across all trims, enhancing its capability in various driving conditions. The Compass offers a comfortable interior with seating for five and a cargo capacity of 27.2 cubic feet. Standard features include a 10.1-inch touchscreen infotainment system with wireless Apple CarPlay® and Android Auto™ integration, heated seats, and a heated steering wheel. Advanced safety features such as forward collision warning, lane departure warning, and blind-spot monitoring are also standard.
