​​The 2025 Škoda Karoq is a compact SUV that combines practicality with modern features. It offers a range of engine options, including a 1.0-liter TSI petrol engine with 115 horsepower, a 1.5-liter TSI with 150 horsepower, and a 2.0-liter TDI diesel engine with either 116 or 150 horsepower, the latter available with four-wheel drive. Transmission choices include a six-speed manual and a seven-speed DSG automatic. The Karoq's interior is spacious, providing 521 liters of boot space with the seats up, expandable to 1,630 liters when folded. Standard features encompass dual-zone climate control, an 8-inch infotainment system with wireless Android Auto and Apple CarPlay, and advanced safety systems like blind-spot warning and rear cross-traffic alert.
