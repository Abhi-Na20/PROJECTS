​The 2025 Toyota bZ4X is a compact electric SUV that blends efficiency with advanced technology. It offers two powertrain options: a front-wheel-drive (FWD) configuration with a 201-horsepower electric motor and an all-wheel-drive (AWD) setup delivering 214 horsepower. Both variants are equipped with a 71.4 kWh lithium-ion battery, providing an EPA-estimated range of up to 252 miles for the FWD model. The bZ4X features a spacious interior with seating for five and a 12.3-inch touchscreen infotainment system that supports wireless Apple CarPlay® and Android Auto™ integration. Safety is paramount, with standard Toyota Safety Sense 3.0, including automated emergency braking and lane-keeping assist.
