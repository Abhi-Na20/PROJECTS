The Peugeot e-208 2020 is an all-electric hatchback that combines stylish design with impressive performance. It is part of Peugeot's push into the electric vehicle market, offering a sustainable and fun driving experience. With modern technology, it provides a smooth, quiet ride and is ideal for urban environments.

  • Make: Peugeot
  • Model: e-208
  • Body Style: Hatchback
  • Fuel Type: Electric
  • Engine: Electric motor, 136 hp (100 kW)
  • Drivetrain: Front-wheel drive (FWD)

Vehicle Description:
The Peugeot e-208 2020 is a sleek, all-electric hatchback that combines modern design with eco-friendly performance. Offering a 136 hp electric motor and a range of up to 211 miles, it delivers a smooth and efficient driving experience. Packed with advanced tech features and safety systems, it’s an ideal choice for urban driving.

Extra Features:

  • Infotainment System: 7-inch or optional 10-inch touchscreen with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto compatibility
  • Advanced Driver Assistance Systems: Lane-keeping assist, adaptive cruise control, automatic emergency braking, and parking sensors
  • Charging: Quick charge options with a 50 kW DC fast charger for 80% charge in about 30 minutes
  • Interior: Digital cockpit with customizable display, premium materials, and a spacious cabin
  • Safety: 5-star Euro NCAP rating with multiple airbags and stability control
  • Climate Control: Dual-zone automatic climate control for added comfort
