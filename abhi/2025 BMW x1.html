​The 2025 BMW X1 is a compact luxury SUV that combines sporty performance with advanced technology and versatile features. It offers two trims: the xDrive28i and the high-performance M35i xDrive. Both trims are powered by a turbocharged 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine, with the xDrive28i producing 241 horsepower and the M35i xDrive delivering 312 horsepower. Both trims come standard with all-wheel drive and a seven-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission. The xDrive28i achieves an EPA-estimated 24 mpg city, 33 mpg highway, and 28 mpg combined, while the M35i xDrive offers 23 mpg city, 31 mpg highway, and 26 mpg combined. Inside, the X1 provides a spacious cabin with a 40/20/40 split-folding rear seat configuration, offering up to 57.2 cubic feet of cargo space. Standard features include a 10.7-inch touchscreen infotainment system with wireless Apple CarPlay® and Android Auto™ integration, synthetic leather upholstery, dual-zone automatic climate control, and advanced safety features such as forward collision warning with automatic emergency braking, lane departure warning, and blind-spot monitoring. The M35i xDrive trim adds sportier elements like an adaptive M suspension, M Sport brakes, a Harman Kardon premium audio system, and additional driver assistance features.
