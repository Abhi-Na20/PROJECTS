​​​The 2025 Renault Captur is a compact SUV that combines modern design with advanced technology and efficient performance. It offers two engine options: the TCe 90, a 1.0-liter three-cylinder turbocharged petrol engine producing 90 horsepower, and the E-Tech Full Hybrid 145, which pairs a 1.6-liter four-cylinder petrol engine with electric motors to deliver a combined 143 horsepower. Both engines are paired with a six-speed manual transmission, and front-wheel drive is standard. The Captur features a spacious interior with seating for five and a modular boot offering up to 616 liters of cargo space. Standard features include a 10.4-inch touchscreen infotainment system with Google built-in, wireless smartphone connectivity, and advanced safety systems such as lane departure warning and automatic emergency braking.
