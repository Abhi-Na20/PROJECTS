​The 2025 Chevrolet Equinox EV is a compact electric SUV that combines affordability with impressive range and features. With a starting price of $33,600, it offers an EPA-estimated range of up to 319 miles for the front-wheel-drive (FWD) model. The Equinox EV is equipped with a spacious 17.7-inch touchscreen infotainment system featuring Google integration, providing seamless connectivity. It also supports DC fast charging, allowing for rapid battery replenishment. Standard safety features include forward-collision warning, automated emergency braking, and lane-keeping assist. The interior is designed for comfort, offering ample seating and cargo space, making it a practical choice for families.
