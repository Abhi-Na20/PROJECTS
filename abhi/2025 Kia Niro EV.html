​The 2025 Kia Niro EV is a compact all-electric SUV that combines efficiency with modern technology. It offers an EPA-estimated range of 253 miles on a full charge, making it suitable for daily commutes and longer trips. Equipped with a 64.8 kWh battery, the Niro EV delivers 201 horsepower and 188 lb-ft of torque, providing a responsive driving experience. Standard features include a 10.25-inch touchscreen infotainment system with wireless Apple CarPlay® and Android Auto™ integration, as well as advanced safety technologies like forward collision avoidance and lane-keeping assist. The cabin is spacious, accommodating up to five passengers with ample cargo space. Additionally, the Niro EV supports DC fast charging, allowing for quick battery replenishment during longer journeys.
