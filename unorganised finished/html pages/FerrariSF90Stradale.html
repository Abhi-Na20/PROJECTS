Ferrari SF90 Stradale Overview

The 2024 Ferrari SF90 Stradale is a high-performance plug-in hybrid that redefines the modern supercar. It features a 4.0L twin-turbocharged V8 engine paired with three electric motors, delivering a combined 986 hp and unmatched driving dynamics. With all-wheel drive and advanced torque vectoring, the SF90 achieves a 0-60 mph time of just 2.5 seconds. Inside, it's as tech-focused as it is luxurious, offering a digital cockpit and multiple driving modes.
