Honda CR-V Hybrid Overview

The 2025 Honda CR-V Hybrid is a compact SUV that combines efficiency with practicality. It features a 2.0-liter Atkinson-cycle inline-4 engine paired with dual electric motors, delivering a combined 204 horsepower and 247 lb-ft of torque. Front-wheel drive is standard, with all-wheel drive available as an option.

The CR-V Hybrid achieves an EPA-estimated fuel economy of 43 mpg city, 36 mpg highway, and 40 mpg combined for the front-wheel-drive model. Inside, it offers seating for five and up to 39.3 cubic feet of cargo space behind the rear seats. Standard features include a 7-inch touchscreen infotainment system with Apple CarPlay® and Android Auto™, as well as the Honda Sensing® safety suite.
