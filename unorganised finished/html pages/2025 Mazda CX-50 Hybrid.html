Mazda CX-50 Hybrid Overview

The 2025 Mazda CX-50 Hybrid is a compact SUV that combines Mazda's signature driving dynamics with Toyota's proven hybrid technology. It features a 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine paired with three electric motors, delivering a combined 219 horsepower and 163 lb-ft of torque. All-wheel drive is standard, ensuring enhanced traction and stability. The CX-50 Hybrid achieves an EPA-estimated fuel economy of 38 mpg combined, offering significant savings at the pump. Inside, it provides seating for five and a cargo capacity of up to 29.2 cubic feet behind the rear seats. Standard features include a 10.25-inch infotainment display with wireless Apple CarPlay® and Android Auto™ integration, heated front seats, and a power rear liftgate. Safety features encompass Mazda's i-Activsense suite, including blind-spot monitoring and rear cross-traffic alert.
