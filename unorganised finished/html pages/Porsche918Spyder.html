Porsche 918 Spyder Overview

The 2015 Porsche 918 Spyder is a plug-in hybrid hypercar featuring a 4.6L V8 engine paired with two electric motors, delivering a combined 887 horsepower. It rockets from 0 to 60 mph in just 2.5 seconds, with a top speed of 211 mph. Its innovative hybrid tech and race-bred engineering make it one of the most iconic cars of the 21st century.

Vehicle Specifications

  • Make: Porsche
  • Model: 918 Spyder
  • Body Style: Convertible
  • Fuel Type: Plug-in Hybrid
  • Engine: 4.6L V8 + Dual Electric Motors (887 hp)
  • Drivetrain: All-Wheel Drive (AWD)
  • Price: $1,000,000

The 918 Spyder was ahead of its time, combining extreme performance with electric efficiency, setting new standards for future supercars.

Premium Features

  • Monocoque carbon fiber chassis
  • 7-speed PDK dual-clutch transmission
  • Rear-axle steering for superior handling
  • Plug-in electric range of ~12 miles
  • Limited production: only 918 units worldwide
