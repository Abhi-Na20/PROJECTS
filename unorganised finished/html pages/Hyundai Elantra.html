2025 Hyundai Elantra Overview

The 2025 Hyundai Elantra is a stylish and tech-savvy compact sedan known for its value, efficiency, and comfort. It features a refreshed exterior with sharp lines and a modern grille, giving it a bold and sporty look. Inside, it offers intuitive tech and a spacious layout ideal for both city driving and long road trips.

The Elantra stands out with its fuel economy, advanced driver assistance systems, and smooth handling. It’s an excellent choice for buyers looking for a balance between affordability and upscale features in a petrol sedan.

Vehicle Specifications

  • Make: Hyundai
  • Model: Elantra
  • Body Style: 4-door Sedan
  • Fuel Type: Petrol
  • Engine: 2.0L Inline-4 or 1.6L Turbocharged Inline-4 (N Line)
  • Drivetrain: Front-Wheel Drive
  • Price: $22,000

The 2025 Hyundai Elantra delivers strong fuel efficiency, especially with its base engine, while the N Line version brings extra power and sporty dynamics for performance-minded drivers.
