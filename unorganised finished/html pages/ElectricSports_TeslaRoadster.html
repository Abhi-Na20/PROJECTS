Tesla Roadster Overview

The 2025 Tesla Roadster redefines electric performance with jaw-dropping acceleration and futuristic styling. Powered by a tri-motor all-wheel drive system, it rockets from 0–60 mph in just 1.9 seconds and offers a maximum estimated range of 620 miles. With a sleek carbon-fiber body and retractable glass roof, the Roadster is built for both extreme speed and everyday elegance. Inside, you'll find a cutting-edge infotainment system, premium audio, and Tesla’s latest self-driving capabilities.
