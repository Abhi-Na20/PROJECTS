Chevrolet Equinox EV Overview

The 2025 Chevrolet Equinox EV is a smart, budget-friendly compact SUV that offers impressive electric range and modern convenience. Starting at $33,600, it boasts an EPA-estimated 319-mile range in its FWD version and includes a large 17.7-inch touchscreen with Google built-in for seamless infotainment. It’s a practical yet futuristic ride, perfect for families.

The Equinox EV is equipped with DC fast-charging support and cutting-edge safety features like forward-collision warning and automated emergency braking, providing peace of mind while on the move.

Vehicle Specifications

  • Make: Chevrolet
  • Model: Equinox EV
  • Body Style: SUV
  • Fuel Type: Electric
  • Engine: 220 HP (FWD) / 300 HP (Dual Motor)
