Kia EV9 Overview

The 2026 Kia EV9 is a bold, all-electric SUV crafted for families who want it all—style, power, and space. With a futuristic design and three-row interior, it blends high-end luxury with impressive performance. Built on the E-GMP platform, it delivers fast charging (10–80% in 25 minutes) and a range of up to 300 miles.

Available in RWD and AWD options, the EV9 also boasts a 12.3-inch digital cockpit, augmented reality HUD, and top-tier safety features like Highway Driving Assist 3 and Smart Parking Assist.
