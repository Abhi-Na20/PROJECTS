Aston Martin Valhalla Overview

The Aston Martin Valhalla is a groundbreaking plug-in hybrid supercar powered by a 4.0L twin-turbo V8 and three electric motors. It delivers 1,064 horsepower, launches from 0 to 62 mph in 2.5 seconds, and reaches a top speed of 217 mph. Limited to 999 units, it represents Aston Martin’s leap into electrified hypercar territory.

Vehicle Specifications

  • Make: Aston Martin
  • Model: Valhalla
  • Body Style: Coupe
  • Fuel Type: Plug-in Hybrid
  • Engine: 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8 + 3 Electric Motors (1,064 hp)
  • Drivetrain: All-Wheel Drive (AWD)
  • Price: $800,000

The Valhalla blends stunning design with cutting-edge performance, redefining Aston Martin’s future with electrification.
