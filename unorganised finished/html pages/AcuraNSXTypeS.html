Acura NSX Type S Overview

The 2022 Acura NSX Type S is a hybrid supercar featuring a 3.5L twin-turbo V6 and three electric motors, producing 600 horsepower. It accelerates from 0 to 60 mph in 2.9 seconds with a top speed of 191 mph. Limited to 350 units, it marks the final evolution of the second-gen NSX.
