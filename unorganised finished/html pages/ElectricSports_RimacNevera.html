Rimac Nevera Overview

The 2025 Rimac Nevera is a revolutionary hyper-EV that redefines performance and innovation. With 1,914 horsepower from four electric motors, it rockets from 0–60 mph in just 1.85 seconds. Built in Croatia, this limited-production car is the ultimate expression of next-gen speed and sustainability.
