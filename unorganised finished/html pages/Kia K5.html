2025 Kia K5 Overview

The 2025 Kia K5 is a mid-size sport sedan that stands out with its dynamic performance and advanced features. It offers a compelling blend of style, comfort, and technology, making it a strong contender in its segment.

With a choice of efficient engines and available all-wheel drive, the K5 delivers responsive handling and a confident driving experience. Its interior is designed with driver-centric controls and premium materials, ensuring both comfort and convenience.

  • Model: K5

  • Engine: 2.5L I-4 Gasoline Direct Injection (GDI) THETA-III engine

The Kia K5 offers a balanced combination of power and efficiency, catering to drivers seeking both performance and practicality.
