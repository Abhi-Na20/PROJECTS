Ford Mustang Mach-E Overview

The 2025 Ford Mustang Mach-E is a performance-driven all-electric SUV that combines the spirit of the Mustang with modern electric efficiency. With its bold styling and cutting-edge technology, the Mach-E offers a thrilling driving experience while maintaining practicality. It comes equipped with a range of battery options, including the 70 kWh and 91 kWh configurations, providing up to 312 miles of range. The Mach-E offers both rear-wheel drive (RWD) and all-wheel drive (AWD) configurations, allowing for excellent performance and handling.

Features like Ford’s SYNC 4A infotainment system, a 15.5-inch touchscreen, and over-the-air software updates enhance the driving experience. The SUV also includes a suite of safety features like Ford Co-Pilot360, offering adaptive cruise control, lane-keeping assist, and automatic emergency braking. The Mach-E’s stylish, comfortable interior includes options for a panoramic sunroof and a premium audio system.
