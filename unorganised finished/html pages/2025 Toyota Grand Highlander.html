Grand Highlander Overview

The 2025 Toyota Grand Highlander is a spacious and family-focused SUV offering three rows of comfort, advanced tech, and powerful performance. Whether equipped with the turbocharged gas engine or the efficient hybrid MAX powertrain, it delivers smooth rides, versatile cargo space, and Toyota’s renowned reliability.
