Corvette Z06 Overview

The 2024 Chevrolet Corvette Z06 is a track-focused American supercar boasting a naturally aspirated 5.5L flat-plane crank V8 engine. Delivering thrilling performance, exotic styling, and cutting-edge tech, the Z06 is built to challenge the best from Europe with raw power and precision engineering.

Vehicle Specifications

  • Make: Chevrolet
  • Model: Corvette Z06
  • Body Style: Coupe
  • Fuel Type: Petrol
  • Engine: 5.5L flat-plane crank V8
  • Horsepower: 670 hp
  • Torque: 460 lb-ft
  • Drivetrain: Rear-Wheel Drive (RWD)
  • 0-100 km/h Acceleration: 2.6 seconds
  • Price: $110,100

The Z06 offers race-proven engineering, with an exotic engine note and head-turning presence on any road or track.
