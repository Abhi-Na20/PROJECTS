BMW X1 Overview

The 2025 BMW X1 is a compact luxury SUV that balances dynamic performance with high-end comfort and innovative tech. Available in xDrive28i and M35i xDrive trims, both models feature a turbocharged 2.0L engine and standard AWD. The M35i xDrive ups the ante with 312 horsepower and performance upgrades like adaptive M suspension and sport brakes. Inside, the X1 features premium finishes and cutting-edge tech for a refined driving experience.

Vehicle Specifications

  • Make: BMW
  • Model: X1
  • Body Style: SUV
  • Fuel Type: Gasoline
  • Engine: 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 (241 hp)
  • Drivetrain: All-Wheel Drive (AWD)

The 2025 BMW X1 blends performance, versatility, and luxury in one elegant compact SUV package.
