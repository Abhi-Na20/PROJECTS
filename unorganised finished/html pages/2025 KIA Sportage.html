KIA Sportage Overview

The 2025 Kia Sportage is a compact SUV that combines modern design with versatile performance. It offers a choice of powertrains, including a 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine producing 187 horsepower, as well as hybrid and plug-in hybrid options for enhanced efficiency. Front-wheel drive is standard, with all-wheel drive available across various trims. Inside, the Sportage features a spacious cabin with seating for five and up to 74.1 cubic feet of cargo space when the rear seats are folded. Standard amenities include an 8-inch touchscreen infotainment system with wireless Apple CarPlay® and Android Auto™ integration, while higher trims offer a 12.3-inch dual panoramic display. Safety features encompass a comprehensive suite of driver-assistance systems, such as forward collision avoidance and lane-keeping assist.
