BMW i8 Overview

The 2025 BMW i8 is a plug-in hybrid sports car that continues to push the boundaries of innovation and design. It features a futuristic carbon fiber body, a turbocharged three-cylinder engine paired with an electric motor, and a combined output of 369 horsepower. The i8 offers all-wheel drive performance, a 0–60 mph time of 4.2 seconds, and up to 18 miles of electric-only driving. The scissor doors, digital dashboard, and sleek aesthetics make it a true icon of sustainable luxury.
