Honda Civic Overview

The 2024 Honda Civic is a compact car that combines efficiency, performance, and modern features. It offers two engine options: a 2.0-liter inline-4 engine producing 158 horsepower and a 1.5-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine delivering 180 horsepower. Both engines are paired with a continuously variable transmission (CVT), and front-wheel drive is standard across all trims. The Civic achieves impressive fuel economy, with the base engine offering up to 35 mpg combined and the turbocharged engine up to 36 mpg combined. Inside, the Civic provides seating for five passengers and offers a cargo capacity of 14.8 cubic feet in the sedan variant. Standard features include a 7-inch touchscreen infotainment system with wireless Apple CarPlay® and Android Auto™ integration, while higher trims offer amenities such as a 9-inch touchscreen, a 10.2-inch digital instrument cluster, wireless charging, and a premium Bose audio system.
