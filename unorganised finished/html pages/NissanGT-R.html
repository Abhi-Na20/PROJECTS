Nissan GT-R Overview

The 2023 Nissan GT-R continues its legacy as a high-performance legend, blending all-wheel-drive grip, twin-turbo V6 power, and a tech-focused interior. Known as "Godzilla" for its monstrous performance, the GT-R delivers supercar thrills in a four-seater package built for both speed and everyday driving.

Vehicle Specifications

  • Make: Nissan
  • Model: GT-R
  • Body Style: Coupe
  • Fuel Type: Petrol
  • Engine: 3.8L Twin-Turbo V6
  • Horsepower: 565 hp
  • Torque: 467 lb-ft
  • Drivetrain: All-Wheel Drive (AWD)

The GT-R is a tech-savvy street and track weapon, offering unmatched performance per dollar and signature Japanese engineering precision.
