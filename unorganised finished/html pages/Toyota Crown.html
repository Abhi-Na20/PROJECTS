2024 Toyota Crown Overview

The 2024 Toyota Crown stands as a bold new take on the modern hybrid sedan, blending the ride height of a crossover with the sleek elegance of a luxury vehicle. With distinctive styling, advanced technology, and two powerful hybrid powertrains, it redefines comfort, performance, and innovation in the premium hybrid segment.

Vehicle Specifications

  • Make: Toyota
  • Model: Crown
  • Body Style: Sedan (Lifted/High-Rider)
  • Fuel Type: Hybrid (Gasoline/Electric)
  • Engine: 2.5L 4-cylinder hybrid OR 2.4L turbocharged hybrid (up to 340 hp)
  • Drivetrain: All-Wheel Drive (AWD)
  • Price: $41,000

With a choice of efficient or performance-focused hybrid systems, the Crown offers an upscale, versatile ride ideal for both city driving and long-distance cruising, all while maintaining Toyota’s trusted reliability and innovation.
