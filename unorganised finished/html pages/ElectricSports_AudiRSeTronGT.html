Audi RS e-tron GT Overview

The 2025 Audi RS e-tron GT is a high-performance electric grand tourer that combines Audi’s refined design language with exhilarating power. With up to 637 horsepower in overboost mode and quattro all-wheel drive, it delivers blistering acceleration—0 to 60 mph in 3.1 seconds—while offering refined luxury and quiet comfort. The RS e-tron GT boasts an 800-volt system for rapid charging and a range of up to 250 miles. It's the perfect mix of speed, sophistication, and sustainability.
