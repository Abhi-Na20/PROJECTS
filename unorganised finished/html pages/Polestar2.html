Polestar 2 Overview

The 2024 Polestar 2 is a premium all-electric sedan that fuses Scandinavian design with high-end performance and eco-conscious materials. Developed by Volvo’s electric performance brand, it features a dual-motor setup that delivers up to 408 horsepower and comes standard with all-wheel drive (AWD).

This luxury EV provides an engaging and sustainable driving experience, offering up to 270 miles of range. With Google Android Automotive OS, it ensures seamless connectivity and intuitive control. Its minimalistic cabin, built from sustainable materials, emphasizes comfort and technology.

Vehicle Specifications

  • Make: Volvo
  • Model: Polestar 2
  • Body Style: Sedan
  • Fuel Type: Electric
  • Engine: Dual electric motors (up to 408 horsepower)
  • Drivetrain: All-wheel drive (AWD)
  • Price: $50,000

The Polestar 2 blends luxury with eco-innovation, offering a distinctive presence in the premium EV segment.

Premium Features

Depending on trim level and options, the Polestar 2 offers the following standout features:

  • Google Android Automotive OS with built-in apps
  • 12.3-inch digital driver display and 11-inch infotainment screen
  • Advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS)
  • Panoramic glass roof
  • High-quality interior with sustainable materials
  • Over-the-air software updates
  • Optional performance pack for enhanced handling
