2025 Nissan Altima Overview

The 2025 Nissan Altima is a midsize sedan that blends modern styling with advanced technology and reliable performance. It’s engineered for comfort, practicality, and everyday driving enjoyment.

Featuring a refreshed exterior with a redesigned front fascia and sleek LED headlights, the Altima delivers style and presence. Inside, it offers spacious accommodations with intuitive tech features like a new 12.3-inch infotainment display and wireless smartphone integration.

Vehicle Specifications

  • Make: Nissan
  • Model: Altima
  • Body Style: 4-door Sedan
  • Fuel Type: Petrol
  • Engine: 2.5L 4-cylinder
  • Drivetrain: Front-Wheel Drive (All-Wheel Drive available)
  • Transmission: Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)
  • Horsepower: 188 hp
  • Fuel Economy: 27 MPG city / 39 MPG highway (FWD)
  • Price: $28,140

The Altima delivers a strong balance of efficiency and comfort, making it a practical and stylish option in the midsize sedan market.

Premium Features

The 2025 Altima includes several premium features designed for convenience and safety:

  • 12.3-inch infotainment touchscreen
  • Wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
  • Wireless phone charging
  • Dual-zone automatic climate control
  • Remote engine start with Intelligent Climate Control
  • Nissan Safety Shield 360 (includes emergency braking, blind spot warning, lane departure warning)
  • Optional sport-tuned suspension (SR trim)
  • Available moonroof
  • Premium audio system
