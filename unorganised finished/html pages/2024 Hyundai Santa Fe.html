2024 Hyundai Santa Fe Overview

The 2024 Hyundai Santa Fe is a mid-size SUV that combines modern design with versatile performance options. It offers a choice of powertrains, including a 2.5-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine producing 277 horsepower and 311 lb-ft of torque, mated to an 8-speed dual-clutch transmission. Front-wheel drive is standard, with all-wheel drive available across various trims.

Inside, the Santa Fe provides seating for up to seven passengers and offers a maximum cargo capacity of 79.6 cubic feet with rear seats folded. Standard features include wireless Apple CarPlay® and Android Auto™ integration, while higher trims offer amenities such as a 12.3-inch touchscreen infotainment system, wireless charging, and a premium audio system. Safety features encompass Hyundai's SmartSense suite, including forward collision avoidance and lane-keeping assist.
