McLaren Artura Overview

The 2024 McLaren Artura represents a bold leap into hybrid performance for McLaren. This plug-in hybrid supercar features a 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 paired with an electric motor for a combined output of 671 horsepower and 531 lb-ft of torque. It rockets from 0-60 mph in just 3.0 seconds and reaches a top speed of 205 mph. With an all-electric range of around 11 miles, it provides brief emission-free drives for urban commutes.

Vehicle Specifications

  • Make: McLaren
  • Model: Artura
  • Body Style: Coupe
  • Fuel Type: Hybrid (Gasoline/Electric)
  • Engine: 3.0L V6 Twin-Turbo + Electric Motor (671 hp, 531 lb-ft)
  • Drivetrain: Rear-Wheel Drive (RWD)
  • Price: $237,500

The Artura is McLaren’s first series-production hybrid, merging cutting-edge performance with improved efficiency and electric flexibility.
