Lamborghini Sián Overview

The Lamborghini Sián is a groundbreaking hybrid supercar that combines iconic Italian design with futuristic performance. Powered by a 6.5-liter V12 engine paired with a 48-volt mild-hybrid system, the Sián produces a staggering 819 horsepower. It accelerates from 0 to 60 mph in just 2.8 seconds, with a top speed exceeding 217 mph. The hybrid system utilizes a supercapacitor rather than a traditional battery, offering faster energy discharge and recharge.

Vehicle Specifications

  • Make: Lamborghini
  • Model: Sián FKP 37
  • Body Style: Coupe
  • Fuel Type: Mild-Hybrid (Gasoline/Electric)
  • Engine: 6.5L V12 + 48V e-motor (819 hp)
  • Drivetrain: All-Wheel Drive (AWD)
  • Price: $3,700,000

The Sián represents Lamborghini’s first foray into electrified vehicles, pushing the boundaries of hybrid tech with unmatched style and performance.

Premium Features

  • Carbon fiber construction with active aerodynamic elements
  • 7-speed ISR (Independent Shifting Rods) transmission
  • Advanced regenerative braking system using supercapacitor tech
  • Exclusive customizable interior with premium materials
  • Limited production: only 63 units worldwide
