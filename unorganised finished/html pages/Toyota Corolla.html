2025 Toyota Corolla Hybrid Overview

The 2025 Toyota Corolla Hybrid continues Toyota's legacy of producing reliable, fuel-efficient sedans. Featuring a refreshed design and upgraded tech, the Corolla Hybrid offers great value for drivers who want to save on fuel while enjoying modern comforts and safety features.This model maintains its smooth ride and practical build, with enhancements like improved infotainment and optional all-wheel drive, making it a smart pick in the hybrid sedan category.

Vehicle Specifications

  • Make: Toyota
  • Model: Corolla Hybrid
  • Body Style: Sedan
  • Fuel Type: Hybrid
  • Engine: 1.8L 4-Cylinder Hybrid
  • Drivetrain: Front-Wheel Drive / Optional AWD
  • Horsepower: 138 hp
  • Torque: 105 lb-ft
  • Fuel Economy: Up to 50 MPG combined
  • Price: $25,000

Perfect for commuters and eco-conscious drivers, the Corolla Hybrid balances affordability, efficiency, and comfort in one sleek package.

Key Features

The 2025 Corolla Hybrid comes equipped with the following standout features:

  • 8-inch touchscreen with Apple CarPlay & Android Auto
  • Toyota Safety Sense 3.0 driver assistance suite
  • LED headlamps and taillights
  • Smart key system with push-button start
  • Available Electronic On-Demand All-Wheel Drive
  • Wireless charging and premium audio system
  • Comfortable seating and spacious trunk space
