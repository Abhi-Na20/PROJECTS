Škoda Karoq Overview

The 2025 Škoda Karoq is a compact SUV that blends practicality with modern tech and versatile performance. It offers engine options like a 1.0L TSI (115 hp), 1.5L TSI (150 hp), and a 2.0L TDI diesel with up to 150 hp and available 4WD. Choose between a 6-speed manual or 7-speed DSG automatic. With up to 1,630L of boot space and advanced infotainment and safety features, the Karoq is made for comfort and convenience.
