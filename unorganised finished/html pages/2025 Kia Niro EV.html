Kia Niro EV Overview

The 2025 Kia Niro EV is a compact all-electric SUV that combines efficiency with modern technology. It offers an EPA-estimated range of 253 miles on a full charge, making it suitable for daily commutes and longer trips.

Equipped with a 64.8 kWh battery, the Niro EV delivers 201 horsepower and 188 lb-ft of torque. Features include a 10.25-inch touchscreen infotainment system with wireless Apple CarPlay® and Android Auto™, as well as safety technologies like forward collision avoidance and lane-keeping assist. The cabin accommodates up to five passengers with ample cargo space. It also supports DC fast charging for quick battery replenishment.
