Renault Captur Overview

The 2025 Renault Captur is a tech-forward compact SUV with sleek European styling and impressive flexibility. It offers two engine options: a 1.0L turbocharged TCe 90 petrol engine producing 90 hp, or an E-Tech Full Hybrid 145 with a 1.6L petrol engine and electric motors for a total of 143 hp. Its spacious, smartly-designed cabin comfortably seats five, while the modular boot delivers up to 616 liters of cargo capacity.

Vehicle Specifications

  • Make: Renault
  • Model: Captur
  • Body Style: SUV
  • Fuel Type: Gasoline
  • Engine: 1.0L TCe 90 (90 hp) or 1.6L E-Tech Hybrid (143 hp)
  • Drivetrain: Front-Wheel Drive (FWD)
  • Price: $21,000

The Captur merges technology, efficiency, and a refined design, making it a smart pick for city drivers and small families.
