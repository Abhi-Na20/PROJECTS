Toyota bZ4X Overview

The 2025 Toyota bZ4X is a compact electric SUV that blends efficiency with advanced technology. It offers two powertrain options: a front-wheel-drive (FWD) version with 201 horsepower and an all-wheel-drive (AWD) setup with 214 horsepower. Both configurations feature a 71.4 kWh lithium-ion battery offering up to 252 miles of range (FWD).

Inside, the bZ4X delivers a modern cabin experience with a 12.3-inch touchscreen infotainment system, wireless Apple CarPlay® and Android Auto™, and seating for five. Safety is prioritized with Toyota Safety Sense 3.0, including emergency braking and lane-keeping assist.
