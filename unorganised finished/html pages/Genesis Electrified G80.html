Genesis Electrified G80 Overview

The Genesis Electrified G80 is a luxury all-electric sedan that blends sleek Korean design with powerful electric performance. As part of Hyundai’s premium brand, this EV emphasizes elegance, sustainability, and advanced technology. With dual motors producing up to 365 horsepower, it features all-wheel drive and delivers a quiet, refined ride.

This premium electric sedan offers over 280 miles of range, fast-charging capability, and a host of advanced comfort and driver assistance features. The Electrified G80 elevates eco-conscious driving with an interior that rivals top-tier luxury brands.

Vehicle Specifications

  • Make: Hyundai
  • Model: Genesis Electrified G80
  • Body Style: Sedan
  • Fuel Type: Electric
  • Engine: Dual electric motors (up to 365 horsepower)
  • Drivetrain: All-wheel drive (AWD)
  • Price: $70,000

Combining elegant aesthetics with sustainability and cutting-edge features, the Electrified G80 is a true standout in the luxury EV market.

Premium Features

The Genesis Electrified G80 includes a wide array of premium amenities and tech:

  • Spacious, high-end interior with premium materials
  • 14.5-inch infotainment touchscreen with navigation
  • Advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS)
  • Active noise cancellation for a quiet ride
  • Dual-zone climate control
  • 3D digital instrument cluster
  • Fast-charging capability with over 280 miles of range
