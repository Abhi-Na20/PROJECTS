2025 Cadillac CT5 Overview

The 2025 Cadillac CT5 is a midsize luxury sedan that offers a compelling blend of performance, technology, and style. With its sleek design and advanced features, the CT5 provides a refined driving experience at a competitive price point.

Under the hood, the CT5 comes standard with a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine producing 237 horsepower, delivering a balance of power and efficiency. For those seeking more performance, an optional 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine is available, offering enhanced power and acceleration.

Vehicle Specifications

  • Make: Cadillac
  • Model: CT5
  • Body Style: Sedan
  • Fuel Type: Petrol
  • Engine: 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4
  • Horsepower: 237 hp @ 5,000 RPM
  • Torque: 258 lb-ft @ 1,500 RPM
  • Transmission: 10-speed automatic
  • Drivetrain: Rear-Wheel Drive (All-Wheel Drive optional)
  • Fuel Economy: 23 MPG city / 32 MPG highway
  • Price: $47,595

The Cadillac CT5 offers a harmonious blend of luxury and performance, making it a strong contender in the midsize luxury sedan segment.
