BMW i4 Overview

The 2025 BMW i4 is a sleek, all-electric luxury sedan that combines high performance with advanced technology. As part of BMW's electrified lineup, the i4 is available in multiple trims, including the performance-oriented i4 M50. This sedan features a fully electric powertrain, with the i4 M50 boasting up to 536 horsepower. It offers both rear-wheel drive (RWD) and all-wheel drive (AWD) options, ensuring a dynamic driving experience.

The 2025 i4 delivers a thrilling electric driving experience with a focus on luxury and cutting-edge technology. It features an impressive electric range, fast charging capabilities, and a spacious, high-tech interior that prioritizes comfort and convenience.

Vehicle Specifications

  • Make: BMW
  • Model: i4
  • Body Style: Sedan
  • Fuel Type: Electric
  • Engine: Dual electric motors (up to 536 horsepower in the i4 M50)
  • Drivetrain: RWD and AWD options
  • Price: $54,000

Known for its sleek design and advanced features, the i4 offers exceptional performance, with a focus on sustainability and efficiency.

Premium Features

Depending on the trim level and model year, the BMW i4 includes these premium features:

  • Large curved touchscreen with iDrive 8 system
  • Fast-charging capability
  • Over 300 miles of range on a full charge
  • Premium interior materials and spacious design
  • Advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS)
  • Available high-quality sound systems
  • Apple CarPlay and Android Auto compatibility
