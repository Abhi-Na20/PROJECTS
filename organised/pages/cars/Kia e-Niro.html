The 2020 Kia e-Niro is an all-electric compact crossover that blends practicality with modern eco-friendly technology. Offering an impressive range and a spacious interior, it delivers a smooth, quiet ride with advanced driver assistance systems and an intuitive infotainment setup. The e-Niro is designed to cater to those seeking an efficient and versatile electric vehicle.

  • Make: Kia
  • Model: e-Niro
  • Body Style: SUV
  • Fuel Type: Electric
  • Engine: Electric motor, 201 hp (150 kW)
  • Drivetrain: Front-wheel drive (FWD)

Vehicle Description:
The 2020 Kia e-Niro is an all-electric crossover offering a 201 hp motor and a range of up to 282 miles. It features advanced safety and tech options, making it perfect for families and eco-conscious drivers. The e-Niro combines practicality with a quiet, smooth driving experience.

Extra Features:

  • Infotainment: 8-inch touchscreen with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
  • Driver Assistance: Adaptive cruise control, lane-keeping assist, forward collision warning, and blind-spot monitoring
  • Charging: DC fast charging capability, 80% charge in about 54 minutes (with a 100 kW charger)
  • Interior: Spacious cabin with premium materials, cargo space, and comfortable seating
  • Safety: 5-star Euro NCAP rating, multiple airbags, and stability control
  • Climate Control: Dual-zone automatic climate control
