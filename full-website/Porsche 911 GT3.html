The 2025 Porsche 911 GT3 is a precision-engineered sports car that combines track-ready performance with everyday drivability. Its iconic design and aggressive stance are complemented by a naturally aspirated 4.0-liter flat-six engine that delivers an exhilarating 502 horsepower. With a focus on lightweight construction and aerodynamics, the GT3 offers an unmatched driving experience, whether on the road or the racetrack. The rear-wheel-drive system and advanced suspension setup ensure exceptional handling and agility. Inside, the cabin is focused on the driving experience, with minimal distractions and high-quality materials. The 911 GT3 is a true enthusiast’s car, offering performance and precision in every aspect.

The Porsche 911 GT3 is a high-performance sports car designed for enthusiasts, offering razor-sharp handling and track-ready precision.
