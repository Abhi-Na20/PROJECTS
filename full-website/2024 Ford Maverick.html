​​​The 2024 Ford Maverick is a compact pickup truck that combines versatility and efficiency. It offers a choice between a fuel-efficient hybrid powertrain and a more powerful turbocharged gasoline engine. The Maverick's compact size makes it highly maneuverable, while its 4.5-foot bed and seating for up to five passengers provide practicality for daily use and weekend adventures. With a starting MSRP of $23,400, it offers a range of trims and options to suit various preferences and budgets.

  • Make: Ford
  • Model: Maverick
  • Body Style: Pickup Truck
  • Fuel Type: Gasoline (Hybrid option available)​​
  • Engine: Hybrid: 2.5L Atkinson-cycle I4 engine paired with an electric motor, delivering a combined 191 horsepower.​​
  • Drivetrain: Front-Wheel Drive (AWD)

Vehicle Description:
The 2024 Ford Maverick offers a blend of efficiency, capability, and affordability, making it a compelling choice for those seeking a versatile compact pickup.

Extra Features:

  • Towing Capacity: Up to 4,000 pounds with the available Tow package.
  • Payload Capacity: Maximum of 1,500 pounds.
  • Fuel Efficiency: Hybrid model achieves an EPA-estimated 40 mpg city and 33 mpg highway; gasoline model achieves 23 mpg city and 30 mpg highway.​​
  • Infotainment: Standard 8-inch touchscreen with Ford's SYNC system, featuring Apple CarPlay and Android Auto compatibility.​
  • Safety Features: Ford Co-Pilot360 includes Pre-Collision Assist with automatic emergency braking and auto high beams.
