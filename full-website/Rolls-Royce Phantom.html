The 2025 Rolls-Royce Phantom is a full-size luxury sedan that combines classic elegance with modern performance. It’s powered by a 6.75-liter V12 twin-turbocharged engine, generating 563 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque. The Phantom features an all-wheel drive drivetrain paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission for a smooth and powerful ride. Inside, the cabin offers a bespoke interior with luxurious materials and custom options. Notable features include the iconic illuminated Spirit of Ecstasy, Starlight Headliner, advanced 360-degree cameras, and active noise cancellation. The Phantom also includes cutting-edge safety technology like night vision and adaptive cruise control. This majestic sedan sets the standard for automotive luxury.

  • Make: Rolls-Royce
  • Model: Phantom
  • Body Style: Sedan
  • Fuel Type: Gasoline
  • Engine: 6.75L V12 Twin-Turbocharged engine
  • Drivetrain: All-Wheel Drive (AWD)

Vehicle Description:
The 2025 Rolls-Royce Phantom redefines automotive luxury, offering an unparalleled combination of power, style, and comfort for the most discerning drivers.

Extra Features:

  • Bespoke Interior: A vast array of customization options, including hand-crafted wood veneers, luxurious leather, and the finest materials for upholstery and finishing.
  • Starlight Headliner: Over 1,300 fiber-optic lights embedded in the roof liner, simulating a starry night sky, adding an ethereal touch to the cabin.
  • Advanced Infotainment System: A fully integrated 12.3-inch digital display with a Bespoke audio system, tailored to deliver an immersive listening experience.
  • Night Vision and 360-Degree Camera System: State-of-the-art technology to enhance safety and visibility, especially during night driving.
  • Active Noise Cancellation: Rolls-Royce’s meticulous attention to detail ensures that the cabin remains tranquil with advanced noise-reduction technologies.
  • Driver Assistance Features: Includes adaptive cruise control, lane-keeping assist, and a high-definition 360-degree camera system, providing added convenience and safety.
