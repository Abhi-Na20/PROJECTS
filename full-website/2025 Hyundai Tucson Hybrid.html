​The 2025 Hyundai Tucson Hybrid is a compact SUV that combines efficiency with modern features. It is powered by a 1.6-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine paired with an electric motor, delivering a combined output of 231 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. Front-wheel drive is standard, with all-wheel drive available as an option. The Tucson Hybrid achieves an EPA-estimated fuel economy of 38 mpg combined. Inside, it offers seating for five and up to 74.5 cubic feet of cargo space with the rear seats folded. Standard features include a 12.3-inch touchscreen infotainment system with wireless Apple CarPlay® and Android Auto™ integration, as well as Hyundai SmartSense safety technologies like forward collision avoidance and lane-keeping assist.
