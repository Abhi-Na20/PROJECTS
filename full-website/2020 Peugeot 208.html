The 2020 Peugeot 208 is a stylish and compact hatchback that combines sleek design with excellent fuel efficiency. It’s available with a range of engine options, including a 1.2L 3-cylinder petrol engine and an all-electric variant, making it a great choice for both city driving and longer trips. The petrol engines offer a balance of performance and economy, while the electric model provides an eco-friendly, quiet ride with a range of up to 211 miles on a single charge. Inside, the 208 boasts a modern, driver-focused cabin, complete with a 10-inch touchscreen, Apple CarPlay, and Android Auto integration. The car also includes Peugeot’s i-Cockpit, offering an innovative digital display and ergonomic controls. Safety features include lane-keeping assist, automatic emergency braking, and blind-spot monitoring. With its compact size, advanced tech, and a choice of powertrains, the 2020 Peugeot 208 is perfect for those seeking a versatile and fun-to-drive city car.
