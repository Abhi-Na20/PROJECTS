​The 2025 Ford F-150 Lightning is an all-electric pickup truck that combines Ford's iconic design with advanced electric technology. It offers two battery options: a standard 98 kWh battery providing up to 240 miles of range, and an extended 131 kWh battery offering up to 320 miles. Both configurations feature dual electric motors delivering 452 horsepower and 775 lb-ft of torque, with an optional upgrade to 580 horsepower in the extended-range model. All-wheel drive is standard, ensuring robust performance. The F-150 Lightning supports substantial towing capacities, reaching up to 10,000 pounds with the Max Trailer Tow Package, and offers a payload capacity of up to 2,235 pounds. Its interior is equipped with a 12-inch infotainment display, heated front seats, and advanced safety features such as lane-keeping assist and blind-spot monitoring.

  • Model: F-150 Lightning
  • Body Style: Pickup Truck
  • Fuel Type: Electric​
  • Engine: Dual Electric Motors (452 hp; 580 hp with extended-range battery)
