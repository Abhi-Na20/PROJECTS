​​The 2024 Nissan Titan is a full-size pickup truck that combines robust performance with modern features and versatility. It is powered by a 5.6-liter V8 engine producing 400 horsepower and 413 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission. The Titan offers a maximum towing capacity of 9,290 pounds and a payload capacity of up to 1,600 pounds, making it suitable for various hauling needs. Inside, the cabin provides seating for up to six passengers, with standard features including an 8-inch touchscreen infotainment system with NissanConnect, wireless Bluetooth® connectivity, and compatibility with Android Auto™ and Apple CarPlay®. Safety features encompass blind-spot monitoring, lane departure warning, and a rearview camera, ensuring a secure driving experience.
