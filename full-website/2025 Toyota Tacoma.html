​​​The 2025 Toyota Tacoma is a midsize pickup truck that blends rugged off-road capabilities with modern amenities. It offers a choice of powertrains, including a 2.4-liter turbocharged inline-four engine and an available hybrid option, delivering a combined 326 horsepower and 465 lb-ft of torque. The Tacoma's towing capacity reaches up to 6,800 pounds, and it provides a maximum payload capacity of 1,709 pounds, depending on configuration. Inside, the cabin features a 7-inch digital instrument cluster, an 8-inch touchscreen with wireless Apple CarPlay® and Android Auto™, and available upgrades like a 14-inch touchscreen and JBL audio system. Safety features include Toyota Safety Sense™ 3.0, Blind Spot Monitor, and Rear Cross-Traffic Alert.

  • Model: Tacoma
  • Body Style: Pickup Truck

  • Engine: 2.4L Turbocharged Inline-Four with Electric Motor

The 2025 Toyota Tacoma combines robust performance, impressive towing capabilities, and modern technology, making it a versatile choice for both work and adventure.
