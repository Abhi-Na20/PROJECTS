​​The 2024 RAM 1500 is a full-size pickup truck that combines robust performance with modern features and versatility. It offers a range of engine options, including the 3.6L Pentastar® V6, the 5.7L HEMI® V8, and the 6.2L supercharged HEMI® V8 exclusive to the TRX model. The 5.7L HEMI® V8 with eTorque mild hybrid technology enhances efficiency and towing capabilities. Inside, the RAM 1500 boasts a spacious cabin with high-quality materials, offering amenities like heated and ventilated seats, dual-zone temperature control, and a leather-wrapped steering wheel. Advanced technology features include the Uconnect infotainment system with a touchscreen display, wireless connectivity, and available navigation. Safety features encompass adaptive cruise control, blind-spot monitoring, and a rearview camera, ensuring a secure driving experience.

  • Make: RAM
  • Model: 1500
  • Body Style: Pickup Truck
  • Fuel Type: Gasoline
  • Engine: 3.6L Pentastar® V6
  • Drivetrain: Rear-Wheel Drive (RWD)

Vehicle Description:
The 2024 RAM 1500 combines powerful engine options, impressive towing capabilities, and a suite of modern amenities, making it a versatile and reliable choice for both work and leisure.

Extra Features:

  • Towing Capacity: Up to 12,750 pounds with the 5.7L HEMI® V8
  • Payload Capacity: Up to 2,320 pounds
  • Fuel Efficiency: Approximately 20 mpg city / 26 mpg highway with the 3.6L V6​
  • Infotainment: Uconnect system with touchscreen, wireless Apple CarPlay® and Android Auto™
  • Safety: Adaptive cruise control, blind-spot monitoring, rearview camera​
