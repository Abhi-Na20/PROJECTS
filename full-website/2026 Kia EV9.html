The 2026 Kia EV9 is a bold, all-electric SUV designed for those seeking both space and performance. With a futuristic design and a spacious three-row interior, it offers the perfect balance of luxury and utility for families. Powered by a high-capacity 77.4 kWh battery, the EV9 provides an impressive range of up to 300 miles on a full charge. The vehicle is built on the Electric Global Modular Platform (E-GMP), ensuring quick charging, with a 10-80% charge in just 25 minutes. Available in both rear-wheel drive (RWD) and all-wheel drive (AWD), it delivers powerful performance while maintaining efficiency. Key features include a 12.3-inch digital cockpit, augmented reality head-up display, and a suite of advanced safety technologies like Highway Driving Assist 3 and Smart Parking Assist. The EV9 offers premium comfort with an optional panoramic sunroof and luxurious materials throughout the cabin.
