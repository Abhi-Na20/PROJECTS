​​The 2025 RAM 1500 REV is an all-electric pickup truck that combines classic RAM styling with advanced electric technology. It features dual 250-kilowatt electric drive modules (EDMs), one on each axle, providing all-wheel drive capability. The standard 168 kilowatt-hour battery pack offers a targeted range of up to 350 miles, while an optional 229 kilowatt-hour battery extends the range to up to 500 miles. Both battery options support 800-volt DC fast charging at up to 350 kW, enabling rapid recharging. The 1500 REV boasts a towing capacity of up to 14,000 pounds and a maximum payload of 2,700 pounds, ensuring it meets the demands of both work and leisure. Inside, it offers a modern cabin equipped with advanced technology, including a 14.5-inch touchscreen infotainment system and available hands-free driving assist features.

  • Make: RAM
  • Model: 1500 REV
  • Body Style: Pickup Truck
  • Fuel Type: Electric​
  • Engine: Dual 250-kilowatt Electric Drive Modules (654 horsepower, 620 lb-ft torque)​​
  • Drivetrain: All-Wheel Drive (AWD)

Vehicle Description:
The 2025 RAM 1500 REV seamlessly blends traditional pickup capabilities with modern electric vehicle technology, offering impressive range, towing, and payload capacities, along with advanced features for a versatile driving experience. .

Extra Features:

  • 800-volt DC fast charging at up to 350 kW
  • Vehicle-to-vehicle, vehicle-to-home, and vehicle-to-grid bi-directional charging
  • Up to 7.2 kW power panel in the bed and 3.6 kW in the frunk​
  • 17.7-inch touchscreen with wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto​
  • Five-mode adjustable ride height​
