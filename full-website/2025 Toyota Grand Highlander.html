​​​​The 2025 Toyota Grand Highlander is a mid-size SUV that offers a blend of performance, efficiency, and versatility. It provides three powertrain options: a 2.4-liter turbocharged gasoline engine producing 265 horsepower, a 2.5-liter hybrid system generating 245 horsepower, and the high-performance Hybrid MAX delivering 362 horsepower. All-wheel drive is available across various trims, enhancing traction and stability. The Grand Highlander features three-row seating, accommodating up to eight passengers, with ample cargo space of up to 97.5 cubic feet when the rear seats are folded. Standard amenities include a 12.3-inch touchscreen infotainment system with wireless Apple CarPlay® and Android Auto™ integration, while higher trims offer additional luxury features such as leather upholstery and a panoramic moonroof.
