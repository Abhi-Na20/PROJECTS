​The 2025 Rivian R1T is an all-electric pickup truck that combines rugged capability with impressive performance. It offers multiple powertrain configurations, including a dual-motor setup producing 533 horsepower, a tri-motor option with 850 horsepower, and a quad-motor variant delivering 1,025 horsepower. The R1T features a unibody construction with an integrated battery pack, providing a low center of gravity and robust structural rigidity. All-wheel drive is standard across all models, ensuring exceptional traction and stability. The truck boasts a maximum towing capacity of up to 11,000 pounds and a payload capacity of 1,764 pounds, making it suitable for both work and recreation. Its interior is equipped with a 15.6-inch touchscreen infotainment system, premium materials, and versatile storage solutions, including a front trunk, gear tunnel, and a 54.2-inch bed.
