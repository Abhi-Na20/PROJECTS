The 2020 BMW 330e blends BMW's renowned driving dynamics with the efficiency of plug-in hybrid technology. This sleek and stylish sedan features a 2.0L 4-cylinder engine paired with an electric motor, delivering impressive acceleration and a smooth, responsive drive. The 330e offers an all-electric range of up to 22 miles, with the gasoline engine kicking in for longer trips, making it ideal for both city commutes and highway driving. Inside, the cabin boasts a premium, tech-rich environment with a high-quality infotainment system, including a 10.25-inch display with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto integration. Safety is a priority with standard features like forward collision warning and automatic emergency braking. With an efficient yet powerful powertrain, the 330e strikes a perfect balance between performance and sustainability.
