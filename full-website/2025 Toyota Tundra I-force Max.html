​​​​The 2025 Toyota Tundra i-FORCE MAX is a full-size pickup truck that combines robust performance with advanced hybrid technology. It features a 3.4-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine paired with an electric motor, delivering a combined 437 horsepower and 583 lb-ft of torque. This powertrain enables a towing capacity of up to 11,450 pounds and a payload capacity ranging from 1,575 to 1,680 pounds, depending on configuration. The Tundra i-FORCE MAX achieves an EPA-estimated fuel economy of 20 mpg city, 24 mpg highway, and 22 mpg combined. Inside, it offers a spacious cabin with premium materials, including semi-aniline leather-trimmed seats, and advanced technology such as a 14-inch touchscreen multimedia display with Apple CarPlay® and Android Auto™ compatibility.
