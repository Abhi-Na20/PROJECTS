​​​​​The 2025 Lexus NX is a compact luxury SUV that combines refined design with advanced technology and versatile performance. It offers a range of powertrains, including a 2.5-liter inline-4 engine producing 203 horsepower, a 2.4-liter turbocharged inline-4 delivering 275 horsepower, a 2.5-liter hybrid system with 240 horsepower, and a plug-in hybrid variant offering 304 horsepower and an electric-only range of up to 37 miles. Front-wheel drive is standard, with all-wheel drive available across various trims. Inside, the NX provides seating for five and up to 46.9 cubic feet of cargo space with rear seats folded. Standard features include a 9.8-inch touchscreen infotainment system with wireless Apple CarPlay® and Android Auto™ integration, while higher trims offer amenities like a 14-inch touchscreen, a digital rearview mirror, and a wireless charging pad.
