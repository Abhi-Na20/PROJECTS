​​​​The 2024 Ford F-150 is a full-size pickup truck that combines robust performance with advanced technology and versatility. It offers a refreshed exterior design, featuring a new front grille and updated taillights. Under the hood, the F-150 provides a range of engine options, including a 2.7-liter twin-turbocharged V6, a 5.0-liter V8, and a 3.5-liter EcoBoost V6. The hybrid variant, known as the PowerBoost, combines a 3.5-liter EcoBoost V6 with an electric motor, delivering 430 horsepower and 570 lb-ft of torque. Inside, the cabin is equipped with modern amenities, such as a standard 12-inch touchscreen with Ford's SYNC 4 system, wireless Apple CarPlay® and Android Auto™ compatibility, and available features like Pro Power Onboard, providing up to 7.2 kW of power for tools and devices. Safety features include Ford Co-Pilot360™, offering driver-assistance technologies like automatic emergency braking and lane-keeping assist.

The 2024 Ford F-150 combines powerful engine options, impressive towing capabilities, and modern technology, making it a versatile and reliable choice for both work and leisure.
