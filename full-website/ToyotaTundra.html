​​​The 2024 Toyota Tundra is a full-size pickup truck that combines powerful performance with modern features and versatility. It offers a choice of engine options, including a 3.4-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine delivering 348 horsepower and 405 lb-ft of torque. The Tundra's towing capacity reaches up to 12,000 pounds, and it provides a maximum payload capacity of 1,940 pounds, making it suitable for heavy-duty tasks. Inside, the cabin features a 14-inch touchscreen with wireless Apple CarPlay® and Android Auto™ compatibility, enhancing connectivity. Safety is prioritized with Toyota Safety Sense 3.0, offering a suite of driver-assistance features.

  • Make: Toyota
  • Model: Tundra
  • Body Style: Pickup Truck
  • Fuel Type: Gasoline
  • Engine: 3.4L Twin-Turbo V6
  • Drivetrain: Rear-Wheel Drive (RWD)

Vehicle Description:
The 2024 Toyota Tundra combines robust performance, impressive towing capabilities, and modern amenities, making it a strong contender in the full-size pickup segment.

Extra Features:

  • Infotainment: 14-inch touchscreen with Toyota Audio Multimedia system; wireless Apple CarPlay® and Android Auto™​
  • Safety: Toyota Safety Sense 3.0 suite, including pre-collision system, lane departure alert, and adaptive cruise control​
