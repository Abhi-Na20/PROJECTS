The 2020 Ford Fusion is a midsize sedan that offers a stylish design, impressive performance, and a wide range of tech features. Available in hybrid, plug-in hybrid, and traditional gasoline powertrains, it delivers versatility and efficiency to suit different needs. The Fusion comes with a 2.5L 4-cylinder engine in the base model, while higher trims offer a turbocharged 1.5L or 2.0L engine for more power. The hybrid version offers excellent fuel economy, making it an eco-friendly option for daily commutes. Inside, the Fusion offers a comfortable, spacious cabin with modern technology like an 8-inch touchscreen with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto integration. Standard safety features include Ford Co-Pilot360, which offers a suite of driver-assistance technologies such as pre-collision assist, lane-keeping system, and blind-spot monitoring. With a smooth ride, excellent technology, and a variety of powertrains, the Ford Fusion is an excellent option for those seeking a balance of performance and practicality.
