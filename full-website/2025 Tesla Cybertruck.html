​​The 2025 Tesla Cybertruck is an all-electric pickup that combines Tesla's cutting-edge technology with rugged utility. It features a distinctive angular design and a durable stainless steel exoskeleton. Available in dual-motor all-wheel drive (AWD) and tri-motor AWD configurations, the Cybertruck delivers impressive performance, with the tri-motor variant accelerating from 0 to 60 mph in just 2.6 seconds. It offers a towing capacity of up to 11,000 pounds and a payload capacity of 2,500 pounds, making it both a powerful and practical choice for various hauling needs. The interior boasts a minimalist design with seating for five, complemented by advanced technology features such as an 18.5-inch central touchscreen and a 9.4-inch rear touchscreen. The Cybertruck is also equipped with Tesla's Autopilot system, enhancing safety and driving assistance capabilities.
