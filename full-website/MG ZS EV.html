The 2020 MG ZS EV is an all-electric compact SUV that offers a great balance of affordability, style, and practicality. With a 44.5 kWh battery, it provides a respectable range and is equipped with a host of modern features, making it a strong contender in the electric vehicle market. It also provides a comfortable interior and advanced safety technology.

Vehicle Description:
The 2020 MG ZS EV is an affordable all-electric SUV with a 143 hp motor and a range of up to 163 miles. It comes with advanced tech, safety features, and a spacious interior, making it a practical option for families. The ZS EV offers great value for those looking for an electric vehicle with modern features.

  • Safety: 5-star Euro NCAP rating, multiple airbags, and stability control
