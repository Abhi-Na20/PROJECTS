​​​The 2025 GMC Hummer EV is an all-electric pickup truck that combines iconic design with cutting-edge technology. It offers impressive performance, with the tri-motor variant delivering up to 830 horsepower and 11,500 lb-ft of torque, enabling a 0 to 60 mph acceleration in approximately 3.5 seconds. The Hummer EV features a robust towing capacity of up to 7,500 pounds and a payload capacity of 1,300 pounds, making it suitable for both work and recreation. Its interior is designed for comfort and convenience, offering seating for five, advanced infotainment systems, and a suite of safety features.
