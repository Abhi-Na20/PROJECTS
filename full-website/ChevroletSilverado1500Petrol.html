​​​​​The 2024 Chevrolet Silverado EV is an all-electric pickup truck that combines robust performance with innovative features. Available trims include Work Truck (WT), LT, RST, and Trail Boss, catering to various preferences and needs. The Silverado EV offers impressive range options, with the Work Truck variant providing up to 492 miles per charge, and the RST Max Range version offering up to 460 miles. All models feature all-wheel drive, ensuring enhanced traction and stability. Towing capacity reaches up to 12,500 pounds, with a payload capacity of up to 1,800 pounds, making it suitable for both work and recreational activities. Inside, the Silverado EV boasts a 17.7-inch touchscreen infotainment system, an 11-inch digital driver display, and advanced safety features, including Super Cruise hands-free driving technology.
